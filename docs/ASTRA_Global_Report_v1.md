# 1. Executive Summary  

The ASTRA CORE™ Global Decision Intelligence Index provides a structured and quantitative framework for evaluating sovereign decision integrity across five critical domains:

- Cyber Stability  
- AI Governance Control  
- Legal Coherence  
- Economic Resilience  
- Social Stability  

This report presents a comparative and longitudinal analysis of global decision systems across major economies.

### Key Findings:

- Global leadership is increasingly defined by **cyber and AI governance capability**  
- **Legal coherence** remains the strongest predictor of long-term stability  
- **Social stability** is the most common limiting factor across both developed and emerging systems  

---

# 2. Methodological Overview  

The ASTRA CORE™ index computes Decision Integrity (D) using a weighted multi-variable model:

Dᵢ = 0.25Cᵢ + 0.20Aᵢ + 0.20Lᵢ + 0.20Eᵢ + 0.15Sᵢ  

Where:

- C = Cyber Stability  
- A = AI Governance  
- L = Legal Coherence  
- E = Economic Resilience  
- S = Social Stability  

All variables are normalized to ensure cross-country comparability.
